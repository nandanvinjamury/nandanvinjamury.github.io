About Me

Hi, I'm Nandan, a third year undergraduate senior at Cal Poly Pomona. I am pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science, and aim to work in a professional software engineering position at a game development company. Recognizing the necessary qualifications required within a strong candidate, I have built this website to convey my abilities at a capacity where I may be hired as a professional or an intern. Feel free to examine the rest of this website to learn more!

Hobbies and Interests

When I have time to experiment, I enjoy cooking and tasting flavorful food. Additionally, I am deeply invested in playing video games (especially League of Legends) that inspire me to create based on their design. Other interests I have include writing stories, singing classical Indian Carnatic music, sketching pieces of art, watching movies and sports, playing tennis, and going to the beach.
